Welcome to Motlokoa R's Tutoring Services!

Are you or your child in need of academic support and guidance? Look no further! We offer comprehensive tutoring services for a wide range of subjects and grade levels, both online and in person. With years of experience and a passion for education, we are here to help you succeed.

Our Subjects and Grade Levels

Whether you're a a younger student in Grades 1-3 seeking assistance with Home Language, English First Additional Language, Mathematics, and Life Skills or high school student aiming to excel in subjects like Mathematical Literacy, English, Geography, Life Sciences, Economics, Tourism, Business Studies, or History, we have you covered. For Grades 4-6, we offer support in Home Language, English First Additional Language, Life Skills, Mathematics, Natural Sciences and Technology, and Social Sciences. And for Grades 7-9, we tutor in IsiZulu Home Language, English First Additional Language, Creative Arts, Economics Management and Science, Life Orientation, Mathematics, Natural Science, Social Science, and Technology.

Safe and Convenient Learning

For in-person classes, we provide a safe and conducive learning environment at our venue, ensuring that students can focus on their studies without distractions. For those who prefer online tutoring, we utilize Zoom to create an interactive virtual classroom experience, making learning accessible from the comfort of your home.

Enrollment Information

We understand the importance of planning ahead. That's why applications to join our classes open every December for the upcoming academic year. This allows you to secure your spot and ensure you or your child receives the academic support needed to thrive.
